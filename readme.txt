Welcome to MediuMerge!
-------------------------------------------------------

Website: https://wpeazy.com
Email: support@wpeazy.com

Focused on avid readers, MediuMerge has got a reader centric design similar to the popular blogging platform, Medium. The content is centered to avoid distractions with the clean and minimal design. The built-in powerful read time calculator lets automatically add the ‘minutes to read’ based on the length of content.

-------------------------------------------------------
Features:
-------------------------------------------------------

- Light, simple, minimal
- Dynamic Read minutes Calculator
- Save directly to pocket (requires you to login to pocket)
- Performance optimised
- Reader-focused theme
- Grid layout
- Custom Logo import along with Site title and Tagline
- Content centric - No-widgets No-sidebar
- Responsive
- Bootstrap 4


Note: The theme requires at least 4 featured posts and 6 general posts to display the grid layout perfect in the homepage. The featured post option is given to the end of the post body section.
